Extract of Professional Experience

Nasrin Nasiriyani was born in Yazd in 1975. She finished the High School, Mathematics in Yazd and despite she came 28th in the competition to enter any national University in 1994, she chose the City of Yazd, its University and the Faculty of Architecture/Town-Planning. In 2002 she offered her diploma thesis (Technical/Professional Complex for Boys in Yazd), by which she obtained her University Degree. In 2003 she was nominated Member of the Institute of Engineering – Construction of the Region of Yazd. By following the training of the above-mentioned Institute, she succeeded in obtaining a high degree, which validated the orientation for her students' diploma. At the same time the design and supervision for houses in Yazd are included in her professional experience.
