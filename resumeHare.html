Full name : Mahmoud HaeriZadeh
position : Chairman of the board
Education : PhD in Architecture and Town Planning from Ecole Nationale Superieure des Beaux Arts, de PARIS (ENSBA), Instut d'Urbanisme de l'Universite de Paris (I.U.U.P.)

Extract of Professional Experience

Mahmoud Haerizadeh was born in 1937 in Tehran but he spent his childhood in Yazd. After his baccalaureat in mathematics in Alborz High School in Tehran, he left for France. He then, after having acquired his diploma (D.P.L.G.) from ENSBA and the Diploma in Town Planning from I.U.U.P. in 1968 (recognized as PhD in the Ministry of National Higher Education in Iran). With his knowledge of international culture and art, he integrated this knowledge in social and Persian national tasks while keeping his contacts with his motherland. Furthermore, he obtained the certificate of Devastated Zones in 1972 and a certificate given by the Chamber of Commerce, Paris for the International collaboration in architecture and town planning in 1989. Mahmoud Haerizadeh registered in the Ordre d'Architectes in France, the Order of Engineering and Construction in Tehran Region, Franco-Iranian Cultural Association and BANA International Foundation.

Key projects

  • Conception and realization of Central, Cultural, Educational Arsham Foundation in Kerman (actually named Technical Faculty of Kerman's National University.
  • Residential complex for Professors of National University of Tehran.
  • Collaboration with Professor Georges Kandilis (Professor of the Paris Fine Arts School) in the conception and realization for MIRA University, Toulouse, France and Avicenne University in Hamadan, Iran.
  • Conception and realization of Riding School in Kerman.
  • Conceptual design for the National University and a Cultural City – Market with Amphitheatres (1500 and 300 seats) in Maybod, Azad University.
  • Conception and realization for Parasismic Buildings, Embassy of France, Tehran.
